Geography & Location

Gasabo District occupies the northern half of Kigali City and is bordered by:


  • North: Rulindo and Gicumbi Districts

  • East: Rwamagana District

  • South: Kicukiro District

  • West: Nyarugenge District

The district includes major suburbs of Kigali, such as Nyarutarama and Kacyiru, and encompasses sections of the hills surrounding the city.

Economy & Infrastructure


Gasabo District is a significant economic hub, hosting:


  • Government institutions, including the Office of the President and various ministries

  • Commercial centers and markets

  • Tourism and cultural sites

Infrastructure development projects are ongoing to improve transportation, energy, and sanitation services.


Education

The district offers various educational institutions, including:




  • Primary and secondary schools


  • Vocational and technical training centers


  • Higher learning institutions


Healthcare

Healthcare facilities in Gasabo District include:



  • District hospitals


  • Health centers and clinics


  • Community health programs
